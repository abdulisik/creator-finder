Privacy Policy

    Data Collected:
        Public Data: We only store publicly available data, such as links found in video descriptions or creator profile pages.
        Local Storage: Subscriptions and preferences are stored locally in your browser cookies or local storage.
        Analytics: We use Cloudflare's privacy-preserving analytics to monitor basic usage metrics. No personally identifiable information is collected.

    Logs:
        All server logs are automatically deleted after 7 days, as per Cloudflare Workers Logs limitations. These logs are only used for troubleshooting purposes.

    Third-Party APIs:
        We use external APIs (e.g., YouTube) for data importing and authorization. Each API follows its own privacy policies, which are linked during the authorization process.

    No Data Sharing or Selling:
        We do not share, sell, or resell user data. All data stored on our servers is publicly available, and user-specific information is only held locally on your device.

    Data Deletion:
        For GDPR, CCPA, or similar compliance needs, simply clear your browser cookies and local storage to remove all data related to this website.
